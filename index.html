Autosled Payment Guidelines

  • Payment processing starts once delivery is confirmed and the BOL is verified.
  • QuickPay: processed within 24 hours for a 1.9% fee.
  • Standard ACH payments: 5 business days to process.
  • For example, loads dropped off on Thursday may not appear until the following Wednesday.

After the load is delivered and the standard 5-business-day processing period has passed, you may email payments@autosled.com to check your payment status.
